About EduTrack

EduTrack is a modern attendance tracking system designed to simplify the process for both educators and students. Our QR code-based solution eliminates the need for paper-based attendance sheets and manual data entry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to streamline educational administrative tasks, allowing teachers to focus on what matters most: teaching. By automating attendance tracking, we help educational institutions save time, reduce errors, and gain valuable insights into attendance patterns.
